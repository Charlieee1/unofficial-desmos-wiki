3D Linear Algebra Utility Functions

Magnitude/Length of a vector

m_{ag}\left(x,y,z\right)=\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}}

Distance between two vectors/points

d_{ist}\left(x_{1},y_{1},z_{1},x_{2},y_{2},z_{2}\right)=\sqrt{\left(x_{1}-x_{2}\right)^{2}+\left(y_{1}-y_{2}\right)^{2}+\left(z_{1}-z_{2}\right)^{2}}

Dot Product

d_{ot}\left(x_{1},y_{1},z_{1},x_{2},y_{2},z_{2}\right)=x_{1}x_{2}+y_{1}y_{2}+z_{1}z_{2}
